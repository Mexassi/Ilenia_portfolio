about me


A little bit about me

Hi, my name is Ilenia. I am a passionate visual merchandiser with a meticolous eye for details. Internationally trained and with a focus on luxury brands, I have exstensive vm, styling and sales experience as well as a broad knowledge of different strategies and techniques at my disposal to achieve the best results possible in my role. My time in Italy, Spain and Australia has given me a broad knowledge and understading of how to best work on new displays in accordance with guidelines.

about my work

Work Experience

Miu Miu

The main responsibilities that I undertook whilst in the role were liaising, planning and implementing new windows and floor displays with the chief visual merchandiser to maximise appeal of products. I was also in charge of creating new displays in line with seasonal stock replanishment to increase sell through of the collection.

Bottega Veneta

I have had the pleasure of being part of the team at kering group. During my role as a fashion advisor and in store vm I worked to implement and plan windows and displays to company guidelines and standards as well as plan product range and stock plans to best portray the themes of the runway.

Belle Flaneur

In collaboration with the owner of Belle Fleneur I have had the opportunity to create stunning art installation and window displays using mostly reproposed materials diverted from the waste stream.

David Jones

Implement displays which will foster interest in products and services to maximise sales opportunities. Coordinate and monitor merchandise presentation and displays to company standards throught the store, store entry points and windows. Prepare departaments for special promotions, ensuring props and ticketing are set up as direct. Coordinate the setup of weekly and major events.
